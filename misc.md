## Tweet 2 (technical depth)

What's new under the hood:

- AI Video pipeline: storyboard > approve > generate. Your agent directs, Kling generates, you review.
- Connectors: Kling (video + TTS), Gemini (analysis + image gen), OpenAI (image gen). Credential management built in.
- HDR normalization: iPhone footage mixed with AI clips just works. Hable tonemap via zscale.
- Segment-based render: each clip encodes independently, concat with stream copy. No more corrupt outputs from complex filter graphs.
